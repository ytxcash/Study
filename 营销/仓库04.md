## Day 4 - 仓库案例

### 标题

仓库04：一个人管理40万尺仓库，材料再也不乱

---

### 正文

我在鞋城做过财务和仓库管理，负责管理40万尺仓库，其中散货就有25万尺。

以前也很头疼：

- 材料明明在仓库，却找不到
- 找一批材料，要花十几分钟，甚至几个人一起翻
- 有时候以为没货，又重复下单，几天后整理时又找到一堆

这些问题，让时间和成本都浪费不少。

---

### 改变（关键动作）

后来我只做了一件事：

👉 **使用 YTX，入库时把每批材料和具体库位对应起来**

- 这样每批材料都有明确库位
- 库位按排、列、层组合管理
- 每个货架、每个区域一目了然

简单一改，效果立刻明显。

---

### 结果（真实数据）

调整之后：

- 找材料基本不用再翻仓
- 新员工也能几秒钟就上手
- 库存和实际几乎完全一致

以前找一批材料要十几分钟，
现在用系统一查就能定位，
仓库乱象大幅减少，管理压力也轻了很多

---

### 渐进式执行

我们一开始并没有一次性全部改动，而是：

- 先从一部分仓库或一类材料开始
- 慢慢理顺流程，再逐步推广到整个仓库

最终，整个仓库管理变得顺畅有序，找材料再也不头疼。

---

### 行动引导（成交）

如果你仓库也出现：

- 材料找不到
- 库存和实际对不上

评论区打「仓库」，我把现在这套做法分享给你参考

## Day 4 - Warehouse Case

### Title

Warehouse 04: How I Managed a 400,000 sq ft Warehouse Alone — No More Mess

---

### Content

I used to work in a shoe material city, handling both finance and warehouse operations.

I was responsible for a warehouse of around 400,000 sq ft,
with about 250,000 sq ft of loose materials.

Back then, it was frustrating:

- Materials were in the warehouse, but we couldn’t find them
- Finding one batch could take over 10 minutes, sometimes with multiple people searching
- Sometimes we thought materials were out of stock and reordered,
  only to find them again days later

All of this wasted time and money.

---

### The Change (Key Action)

Later, I made just one change:

👉 **Using YTX, we linked every batch of materials to a specific storage location during inbound**

- Every batch has a clearly assigned location
- Locations are structured by row, column, and level
- Every shelf and area is easy to identify

A simple change, but the impact was immediate.

---

### Results (Real Impact)

After the change:

- No more searching through the warehouse
- New staff can get up to speed in seconds
- Inventory matches reality almost perfectly

Before, finding materials took over 10 minutes
Now, a quick system search shows the exact location

Warehouse chaos dropped significantly,
and management became much easier.

---

### Step-by-Step Implementation

We didn’t change everything at once:

- Started with one section or one category of materials
- Gradually improved the process and expanded

In the end,
the entire warehouse became organized and efficient.
