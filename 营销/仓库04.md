It used to be a headache:

- Materials were in the warehouse, but we couldn’t find them
- Finding a batch could take 10+ minutes, sometimes several people digging through
- Sometimes we thought we were out of stock and reordered, only to find a pile a few days later

All this wasted a lot of time and money.

---

## Change (Key Action)

👉 **Later, I switched from the old ERP to YTX**

- Every batch of materials has a clear location
- Storage is organized by row, column, and layer
- Every shelf and area is easy to see at a glance

---

## Results (Real Data)

The impact was immediate:

- Front desks can check materials directly, no more asking the warehouse
  - Takes only 2 seconds to see the location
- Warehouse material retrieval (about 250,000 sq ft of loose stock):
  - A “hand” (batch) takes up to 5 minutes
  - A single sheet takes no more than 30 minutes
- Material handling is faster, overall operations are much more efficient

## Gradual Implementation

We didn’t change the whole warehouse at once:

- Started with the popular series
- Then gradually expanded to other series

In the end, warehouse management became smooth and organized. Finding materials is no longer a hassle, and year-end stocktaking is easy.
