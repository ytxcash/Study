## Title

Warehouse 04: Managing a 400,000 sq ft warehouse alone — no more mess

---

### Body

In the last post, I shared one key action 👇
👉 Link each item to its storage location during receiving — this is exactly what I did

Before that, it was a constant headache:

- The system showed items in stock, but we couldn’t find them
- Finding one item could take over 10 minutes, sometimes with multiple people searching
- We thought items were out of stock, reordered them, then found them days later

All of this wasted a lot of time and money.

---

## Change (Key Action)

👉 **I replaced the old warehouse system with YTX**

- Every batch now has a clear location
- Locations are organized by row, column, and level
- Every rack and area is easy to see and manage

---

## Results (Real Data)

The impact was immediate:

- Sales staff can check stock directly — no need to call the warehouse
- Picking time (around 250,000 sq ft of loose stock):
  - Up to 5 minutes per lot
  - Within 30 minutes per piece
- Overall efficiency improved significantly

---

## Step-by-Step Implementation

We didn’t change everything at once. Instead:

- Started with best-selling lines
- Then expanded to other categories

In the end, the warehouse became organized and easy to manage — no more struggling to find items
