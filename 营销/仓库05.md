## Title

Warehouse 05: How to Bind Materials to Storage Locations in Practice

## Body

Here’s how YTX is designed:

---

## Step 1 – Receiving Materials

👉 **Each batch of materials must be assigned a storage location**
**The storage location is the core node — without it, data cannot be entered**

In other words, when materials arrive, they must **be entered into the system together with their location**.

---

## Step 2 – Warehouse Setup

The warehouse was also standardized 👇

👉 **Each shelf and location is pre-numbered and fixed**

- Example: Row 1 – Column 1 – Level 1
- Aisles are also labeled for easier storage and picking

---

## Key Point

The goal isn’t to “remember where things are”

👉 **It’s to be able to find locations directly in the system**

Once this is in place, picking no longer relies on experience, and new staff can get up to speed quickly
