## 正文

---

## 实际做法（第一步）

👉 **每批材料必须录入库位**
**库位是核心，没有库位就不能录入数据**

也就是说，材料进仓时必须“带着位置”入系统

---

## 实际做法（第二步）

仓库也做了规范化调整👇

👉 **每个货架和位置提前编号并固定**

- 示例：1排-1列-1层
- 通道也要标记，放货更方便

好处：
👉 直接按位置取货，不用问人，效率大幅提升

---

## 关键点

重点不是“记住放在哪”

👉 **而是系统里能直接查到位置**

做到这一点：
找材料不再靠经验，新员工也能迅速上手

## Title

Warehouse 05: How to Bind Materials to Storage Locations in Practice

## Body

This is how YTX is designed:

---

## Step 1 – Receiving Materials

👉 **Each batch of materials must be assigned a storage location**
**the storage location is the core node; without it, data cannot be entered**

In other words, when materials arrive, they must **enter the system along with their location**.

---

## Step 2 – Warehouse Standardization

The warehouse was also organized and standardized 👇

👉 **Each shelf and location is pre-numbered and fixed**

- Example: Row 1 – Column 1 – Layer 1
- Aisles are also labeled for easier placement

Benefits:
👉 Pick items directly by location, no need to ask anyone, efficiency greatly improved

---

## Key Point

The focus isn’t on “remembering where things are”

👉 **It’s about being able to find locations directly in the system**

Once this is done, picking materials no longer relies on experience, and new staff can get started quickly
