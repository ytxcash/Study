# 短视频

## Day 2 - 引

### 标题

仓库02：材料乱，找起来太费时间

---

### 正文

很多鞋材老板都遇到过：

客户下了订单，材料明明在仓库里，
翻了半天找不着。

你以为没货了，还可能重新下了生产单，
结果过几天材料又出来了。

---

### 原因点破

大多数情况不是不用心，而是：

1. 材料位置没有规范
2. 仓库规模太大，传统方法效率低

时间一长，查找效率下降，订单也可能被拖延。

---

### 结尾（留白/悬念）

如果每批材料都有精确的位置记录，
查询的时候，一眼就能看到，
库存管理就轻松多了…

## Day 2 - Hook

### Title

Warehouse 02: Materials Are There… But Hard to Find

---

### Content

Many shoe material business owners have been there:

A customer places an order.
You know the materials are in the warehouse.
But after searching for a while… you still can’t find them.

You think they’re out of stock,
so you place another order…
only to find the materials a few days later.

---

### The Real Problem

Most of the time, it’s not carelessness:

1. Material locations aren’t clearly organized
2. The warehouse is too big for traditional methods

Over time,
searching takes longer and longer,
and orders start getting delayed.

---

### Ending (Open Loop)

But imagine this:
every batch of materials has an exact location.

A quick search,
and you know exactly where it is.

Warehouse management suddenly becomes so much easier.
