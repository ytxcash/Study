## Day 3 - 引 / 成交

### 标题

仓库03：保持整齐有序，只差这一步

---

### 正文

仓库有没有这种情况：

- 材料明明在，就是找不到
- 每次拿东西，经常翻半天

问题就一个👇

👉 **材料没有记录“具体放在哪”**

---

### 核心讲解

很多仓库其实是这样做的

入库只登记数量，放在哪里还记在纸上。

结果就是：👉 用的时候，依旧要去翻。

时间一长，仓库只会越来越乱，越找越慢

---

### 解决思路（铺垫 Day 4）

其实不用一下子大改，
先做好一件事👇

👉 **入库的时候，绑定材料和位置**

做到这一点：

- 找东西不用翻
- 新人也能直接上手
- 仓库开始可控

---

### 降低门槛

可以先选一个小区域试一下，
不用全部推翻重来。

---

### 行动引导

如果您觉得仓库该管一管了，
私信我「仓库」，我把那套落地方法发您。

## Day 3 - Hook / Conversion

### Title

Warehouse 03: Your Warehouse Isn’t Messy — You’re Just Missing This One Step

---

### Content

If you’re facing this situation right now:

- Materials are clearly in the warehouse, but you just can’t find them
- Every time you look for something, you have to search everywhere

Then the problem usually comes down to one thing:

👉 **Material locations are not accurately recorded**

---

### Core Explanation

This is how many warehouses are managed:

- When materials are received, only quantities are recorded
- No precise location is assigned — just “somewhere in the warehouse”

So when you need something,
people have to search manually

Over time,
the warehouse gets messier,
and finding materials becomes harder and harder.

---

### Solution (Leading to Day 4)

You don’t need to overhaul everything at once.

Just focus on one thing:

👉 **Bind each material to a specific location during inbound**

This way,
you can instantly locate materials in the system
instead of relying on manual searching.

---

### Lower the Barrier

You can start small:

- Begin with one warehouse area or one category of materials
- Gradually standardize the process, then expand
