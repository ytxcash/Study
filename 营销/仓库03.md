## Day 3 - 引 / 成交

### 标题

仓库03：保持整齐有序，只差这一步

---

### 正文

仓库有没有这种情况：

- 材料明明在，就是找不到
- 每次拿东西，经常翻半天

问题就一个👇

👉 **材料没有记录“具体放在哪”**

---

### 核心讲解

很多仓库其实是这样做的

入库只登记数量，放在哪里还记在纸上。

结果就是：👉 用的时候，依旧要去翻。

时间一长，仓库只会越来越乱，越找越慢

---

### 解决思路（铺垫 Day 4）

其实不用一下子大改，
先做好一件事👇

👉 **入库的时候，绑定材料和位置**

做到这一点：

- 找东西不用翻
- 新人也能直接上手
- 仓库开始可控

---

### 降低门槛

可以先选一个小区域试一下，
不用全部推翻重来。

## Day 3 - Hook / Conversion

### Title

Warehouse 03: Stay Organized — You’re Just One Step Away

---

### Body

Does your warehouse have these problems?

- You know the materials are there… but you just can’t find them
- Every time you need something, you end up searching for ages

There’s really only one issue 👇

👉 **No record of where items are actually stored**

---

### Key Point

A lot of warehouses work like this:

They record quantities when items come in,
but where things are placed is only written on paper (or remembered).

You still have to dig around to find anything.

Over time, the warehouse gets messier,
and finding things takes longer and longer.

---

### Solution (Preview for Day 4)

You don’t need a big overhaul right away.
Just start with one simple step 👇

👉 **Link each item to its exact storage location during receiving**

Once you do this:

- No more searching around
- New staff can pick things up quickly
- Your warehouse becomes manageable

---

### Keep It Simple

Start small — pick one area and test it out.
No need to change everything at once.
