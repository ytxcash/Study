# Day 3 - 引 / 成交

## 标题

仓库03：保持整齐有序，只差这一步

---

## 正文

仓库有没有这种情况：

- 材料明明在，就是找不到
- 每次拿东西，经常翻半天

问题就一个👇

👉 **材料没有记录“具体放在哪”**

---

## 核心讲解

很多仓库其实是这样做的

入库只登记数量，放在哪里还记在纸上。

结果就是：👉 用的时候，依旧要去翻。

时间一长，仓库只会越来越乱，越找越慢

---

## 解决思路（铺垫 Day 4）

其实不用一下子大改，
先做好一件事👇

👉 **入库的时候，绑定材料和位置**

做到这一点：

- 找东西不用翻
- 新人也能直接上手
- 仓库开始可控

---

## 降低门槛

可以先选一个小区域试一下，
不用全部推翻重来。

## Day 3 – Hook / Conversion

## Title

Warehouse 03: Stay Organized — You’re One Step Away

---

## Body

Does your warehouse have these problems?

- Materials are there… but you can’t find them
- Every time you need something, you have to search

There’s only one reason 👇

👉 **No clear record of where items are stored**

---

## Key Point

Many warehouses work like this:

They record quantities,
but not the exact location.

So every time you need something —
you still have to dig for it.

Over time, it only gets worse.

---

## Solution (Preview for Day 4)

You don’t need a big change.

Just do one thing 👇

👉 **Link each item to storage location during receiving**

Once you do this:

- No more searching
- New staff can pick it up fast
- The warehouse becomes manageable

## Keep It Simple

Start small.
Test one area first.
